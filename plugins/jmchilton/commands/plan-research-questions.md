You will be given a plan file and an optional context about which questions to address.

There will be a list of unresolved or open questions toward the bottom of this plan, your job is to
use subagents to resolve the resolve these questions.

Launch a subagent for each unresolved question to research the question and give you back an answer -
please be sure to supply the subagent with any relevant additional context that is important.

Collect the responses from the subagents and add them into the document as part of the unresolved
questions section. Your job is not to merge their answers into the rest of the document - that will be
a subsequent step.
